DVD Screensaver 2 Demo

Celebrate the 30th anniversary of the DVD with our silly - yet oddly respectful - survivors-like interpretation of the original bouncing DVD logo screen saver. Sit back, choose upgrades and watch it go. If you like the DEMO, the full game is available on Steam for $ 2.99 and icludes lots of fun little features - Like the fact that you can use it as your real Windows screensaver!"

The Demo available in browser features the first of six stages available in the full version, as well as a limited set of upgrades and items. The game is made by Studio Gauntlet - norwegian based indie game dev duo consisting of Christer and Alexander - and based on the original bouncing dvd logo screen saver found in many DVD players from the 2000's. We comissioned Rune Bjerkaas to make the music for this game. DVD Screen Saver 2 is our homage to the DVD, and a silly little survivors excersise where we explore what happens if we make a survivors-like with no movement input. The full game and soundrack is available on STEAM for $ 2.99. Enjoy!
